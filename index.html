01. What is OrangeCoin?
OrangeCoin is based on a meme coin model with some great features but also has a use case to make it a DeFi protocol. On each transaction, a fee is cut which goes to automatic liquidity generation and static rewards to holders for continuous growth.
02. Tokenomics
OrangeCoin has a perfect token distribution and fees plan that charges 5% each on every transaction that goes to static rewards and auto liquidity generation, bringing benefit not only to the token holders but also to the future of the protocol.
03. Liquidity
5% tax on each transaction is cut, half of the tax gets converted to BNB and added with the other half to equally supply the liquidity pool on PancakeSwap automatically locked forever. This function solidifies the future of OrangeCoin.
04. Rewards
Another 5% tax is cut on each transaction and sent to holders divided according to their holding amount. This function is called reflection or static rewards and rewards users just for holding $ORANGE.
05. Charity
We will hold crowdfunding campaigns in the community for important causes and charity organizations. The funds will be stored in a metamask wallet and the wallet address will be shared. Once the wallet has a respectable amount in it, donations will be done transparently.
06. Manual Burning
$ORANGE burning will be a manual process done by the OrangeCoin devs to ensure that the price of the token stays stable or increases. Burning will be done according to a meticulous burn map which involves big milestones and large events.
07. OrangeSwap
OrangeSwap is a decentralized exchange, yield farm, and staking platform deployed on the Binance Smart Chain. OrangeSwap is a modified fork of PancakeSwap, the most famous AMM on the Binance Smart Chain.

Frequently Asked Questions

$OrangeCoin is a deflationary asset that rewards its HOLDRs and ensures the health of its ecosystem through automatic liquidity generation. Hosted on BSC, $OrangeCoin's ecosystem includes all by featuring low gas fees.

On the leading AMM on Binance Smart Chain: PancakeSwap. Expansion is planned to other exchanges as well. Moreover, OrangeSwap will also feature $OrangeCoin.

Any wallet that supports the BEP20 token standard can store $OrangeCoin. Such wallets include, but are not limited to, Trust Wallet and MetaMask. However, simple custom settings need to be applied to these wallets to store $OrangeCoin.

OrangeSwap is a fork of PancakeSwap, capable of always providing liquidity, regardless of how large a trade order is or how small the liquidity pool. The founder of OrangeSwap, Mr. Orange, created $OrangeCoin.

Every transaction involving $OrangeCoin is taxed a 10% fee. Half of this fee gets rewarded back to HOLDRs while the other half gets deposited into the liquidity pool on PancakeSwap. Of the half deposited into PancakeSwap, half is converted to BNB and paired with the remaining $OrangeCoin to create an asset pair. The $OrangeCoins converted to BNB are, in essence, burned forever.

Binance Smart Chain is known for its near-zero gas fees. Ensuring users of OrangeCoin will never have to pay hefty unexpected fees for their transactions.
